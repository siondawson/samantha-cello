Part of Aderyn Strings

As the principal cellist of Aderyn Strings, Samantha offers both solo cello performances and leads the acclaimed Aderyn String Quartet. Established in 2013, Aderyn Strings has become South Wales' premier choice for wedding and event music, combining classical excellence with contemporary style.

Whether you're seeking an elegant solo cellist or the full quartet experience, Aderyn Strings provides versatile musical options for your special day. From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, Samantha and the Aderyn ensemble create bespoke musical experiences across Cardiff, London, and the UK.

About

Samantha Drees is an accomplished musician specialising in cello and violin. With a Bachelor's degree from Baldwin Wallace University and Conservatory, followed by Honours from the Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama, Samantha brings a wealth of expertise to every performance.

As a core member of the Aderyn and Serenade String Quartets, Samantha has enriched numerous private events and public concerts with her elegant renditions. Beyond classical, she also shines on the bass guitar, often collaborating with her husband, himself a talented musician.

Samantha's musical journey has taken her around the globe, including appearances at esteemed festivals like the British Isles Music Festival and the International Music Academy Germany. Her experience extends to the cello section of the Opera de Bauge Festival in Bauge, France.

Playing on a vintage French cello from the 1850s, Samantha's sound is both rich and timeless, adding a touch of sophistication to any occasion. With years of solo performance under her belt, Samantha promises to elevate your wedding day with her artistry and passion.

For a memorable wedding experience filled with beautiful music, consider booking Samantha Drees. Let her talent and expertise create the perfect soundtrack for your special day.
